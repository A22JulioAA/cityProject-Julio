Notable people born in Paris

Edith Piaf

Also known as “The Little Sparrow,” was a French singer who became an icon of France during World War II. (1915-1963)

Nicolas Sarkozy

French politician who served as the 23rd President of France from 2007 until 2012.(1955)

Napoleon III

Nephew of Napoleon I, Emperor of France from 1852 to 1870 and Military leader.(1808-1873)

Paul Gauguin

French painter of the late 19th century, known for his bold colors, primitive style and paintings of young Polynesian girls. (1848-1903)

Napoleon II

Duke; the son of Napoleon Bonaparte, who named him as his successor.(1811-1832)
